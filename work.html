Kerala Relief Fund Donation

pragati swayam sahayata mahila bachat gat have done many donation camps.During the kerala floods their was a similar camp conducted and many people willingly came forward to donate various items such as clothes,grains,medicines etc.apart from all these a check of rupees 10,000 was also donated to the CM charity relief fund.

classes

pragati swayam sahayata mahila bachat gat also conduct wpmen's classes on tailoring,handicraft,cooking etc.all this course are conducted in batches in vasai-nallasopara and the price of this classes is very much affordable for everyone.for further enquiry regarding the course,class place and timing you most welcome to contact us on our social sites.

ACTIVITES

pragati swayam sahayata mahila bachat gat conduct small activities such as yoga-session,drawing activites during republic and independence day for children in the nana-nani park which is also maintained by the bachat gat.many tree plantion are also done regular interval to keep park fresh and clean for citizen.also free health-camps have been also conducted.

social services

pragati swayam sahayata mahila bachat gat president kavita chauhan along with her family have done the cloth face-mask distirbution to our corona warriors and other needy people too.bachat gat actively takes part in various social-activities.an article was also published in newspaper for their work during covid-19 pandemic situation.

Stalls

pragati swayam sahayata mahila bachat gat orgainse and places their stalls which contain the hand-made items made by the women's to whom the bachat gat have provided jobs and help them to make their daily earnings.also bachat gat provides stalls on rent to those women's whose want to sell their product.till now many such stall have been conducted and many more to come.
